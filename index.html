Memory Matrix Game

Icons of Computing

Re-Designed By Dhanush

made with Visual Studio Code for Education

Instructions :-


Match all pairs of cards by flipping them

  • Flip a Card: Click on any card to reveal the symbol.
  • Find a Match: Click on a second card to see if it matches the first one.
  • Match or Miss: If the cards match, they stay revealed and if the cards don't match, they flip back after a short delay.
  • Keep Going: Keep flipping cards until all pairs are matched.

Have Fun, Enjoy the game and test your memory skills!

Congratulations! You found all the pairs!
